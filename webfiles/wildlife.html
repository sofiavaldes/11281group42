General wildlife information:

  Congress Bill: Recovering America's Wildlife Act

This website details the Recovering America's Wildlife Act of 2021 and why it is needed in order to preserve all wildlife species. About one-third of species in America are at a high risk for extinction, and this bill would provide $1.39 billion in annual funds to help this issue.

  Florida Fish and Wildlife

This government organization's webiste is great for learning more about the wildlife in Florida along with the issues they are facing and how you can help. This website also provides information on how to hunt and fish safely and how to get involved with conservation efforts.

  Ocean Conservancy

This organization works to conserve and protect the ocean's ecosystems from today's global challenges. The website has information on how to get involved, daily news stories, and small things you can do to help. They also provide specific information on Florida's coasts and sustainable fisheries.

  Florida Ocean Alliance

This website is for the organization Florida Ocean Alliance. They advocate for Florida's environment through ocean and coastal research, conservation, education, and economic development. They conduct outreach and educational activities, hold conferences, and publish policy papers in order to ensure that Florida's water resources are protected.
Information on sustainable fisheries:

  FL Sea Grant: Sustainable Fisheries and Aquaculture

Florida Sea Grant is a university-based program that supports research and education on Florida's coastal resources. This page provides great information about sustainable fisheries and aquaculture in Florida. It also contains links to other pages that talk about artificial reefs, catch and release, and seafood quality.

  The Nature Conservancy: Promoting Sustainable Snapper and Grouper Fisheries

This article talks about sustainable fisheries and what they are, the impact of recreational fishing in Florida, and the effect of barotrauma. This article also mentions the Nature Conservancy's "Back to Depth" program initiative and how you can get involved.

  Ocean Conservancy: Sustainable Fisheries

This is the Ocean Conservancy's page on sustainable fisheries and it provides a lot of useful information. It talks about the problem with overfishing and how sustainable fisheries could be the solution. It also has tabs for POSEIDON, a project which uses computer modeling to better understand the ecosystem and fisheries management, along with a tab for wildlife fact sheets.

  NOAA Fisheries

This government website contains a ton of information on everything their is to know about fisheries in the U.S. They provide lists of all protected aquatic wildlife species, resources for fishing, information on sustainable fisheries and seafood, marine life conservation, and ecosystem and habitat conservation.
