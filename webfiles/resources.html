Organizations and general information:

  Water Quality Association

WQA is a resource and information source, a voice for the industry, an educator of professionals, and laboratory for product testing.

  USGS: Water Quality Information

This resource answers the question of "what is water quality?" and has information on the basics, groundwater quality, wastewater, and water quality properties.

  Florida DEP: Water Quality Standards

This resource talks about the standards in Florida for its surface water quality. It also provides quick links that have more information on Florida's water quality.

  Protecting Florida Together

This is a Flroida government website that contains details about the different task forces and restoration initiatives that aim to protect and restore Florida's environment.

  Florida DEP: Division of Water Restoration Assistance

This page of the DEP website details the Division of Water Restoration Assistance's role in the improvement of the quality of water resources in the state.

  The Nature Conservancy: Protecting Fresh Water in Florida

This page of the Nature Conservancy's website talks about their vision for freshwater conservation in Florida.
Resources for water quality improvement:

  Princeton Hydro: Water Quality Tips

This resource provides ways you can help improve the water quality in your community by changing some processes in your daily routine.

  South FL Water Management District: What Can You Do

This website contains information about how you can help reduce pollution and protect your community and the Everglades.

  UF IFAS Blog

This blog article from the University of Florida describes what causes poor water quality and how you can play a part in its improvement.
Resources for water quality maintainence:

  EPA: Things You Can Do to Protect Water Quality

This government website provides information and ideas about how you can play a part in maintaining your community's water resources.

  SouthWest Water Company

This website gives tips and advice on how to maintain water quality and protect local water sources.
